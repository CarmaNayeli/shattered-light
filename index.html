About the Game

Shattered Light is a fan tabletop RPG set in the world of Steven Universe. You play as Gems — crystalline beings shaped by Homeworld — who are learning what it means to choose who you are. Maybe you're a Crystal Gem fighting for Earth. Maybe you're still figuring it out.

The game centers on Bonds (asymmetric relationship ratings that power your dice), Fusion (two characters merging their strength and tracking Harmony), and the five stats that define how a Gem moves through the world: Form, Clarity, Resonance, Radiance, and Resolve.

Rolls are pools of d6s. You read the highest. Two sixes is Resonance — something extraordinary happens.

d6 Dice Pools

Roll a number of d6s equal to your stat plus Bond dice. Read the highest: 6 = success, 4–5 = partial, 1–3 = miss.

Resonance

When two or more dice show 6, something extraordinary happens — a Resonance result beyond ordinary success.

Bonds

Track relationships as 0–5 ratings. One Bond per roll can add up to +4 dice when that relationship is genuinely active in the scene.

Fusion

Two Gems can fuse into a new being with its own stats and moves. Track Harmony — it drops on disagreement and recovers through connection scenes.

The Five Stats

Form

Physical capability, combat, and endurance. Form Damage chips away at this stat during fights.

Clarity

Awareness, analysis, memory, and reading other gems or situations.

Resonance

Emotional attunement, empathy, and connection across the bond between beings.

Radiance

Expression, creativity, performance, and the gem's unique light.

Resolve

Willpower, endurance under pressure, and holding to what matters most.
